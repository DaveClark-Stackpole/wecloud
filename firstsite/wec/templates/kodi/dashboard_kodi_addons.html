Useful AddOns

*** Genesis is coming to an end and you need more reliable AddOns ***
*** Below are links to how to add some useful addons. I've listed them in order of effectiveness ***

Exodus (Exodus is HERE !) It's back! This is pretty much Genesis reborn. Probably the best one if you only have one

SALTS (Stream All The Sources) (TV shows-Excellent / Movies - OK) Salts is probably the best TV Show addon. It loads fast and hardly ever buffers. It contains an unbelievable amount of shows and it has great quality.

WATCH 1080p (MOVIES - Excellent) Watch1080p has a great selection and the quality if very good. I haven't had any buffering issues and it's nice having just a few selections to choose from and knowing they all work.

1Channel (TV shows-Very Good / Movies - Good) Primewire's 1 Channel is still one of my favourites. With Genesis going down sometime in the near future this is a very good alternative.

Velocity (TV shows-Very Good / Movies - Good) Velocity is a brand new one and it seems pretty good. It's a nice alternative and offers many of the same features Genesis had.
