Steps to Live TV (Castaway + New Favorites)

(Video for lazy people)

Below are steps to installing Castaway which has many live feeds. Some work and some don't but it's a nice site to pick up live channels. I also put together in the Live2 folder a new Favorites file you can add that has many of the popular channels that do work if you want to save time. Enjoy !


1. Download the zip file Live2 and extract it to a flash drive.

2. Your flash drive should now have two files Live2.zip and favourites.xml (DO NOT UNZIP castaway.zip file)

3. Plug your flash drive into one of your USB slots on your Android Box / OpenElec Raspberry Pi or computer running XBMC. It's recommended your unit be off prior to doing this to avoid unexpected shutdowns.

4. Open XBMC/ Kodi and navigate to SYSTEM then SETTINGS. Then select Add-Ons and choose Install from zip file. Look for your flash drive name and select it then click on castaway.zip..

5. Then, without exiting that screen, click on Get_Add Ons directly above install from zip. Click on natko1412 repo then click on Video-Addons and then Castaway and Install. This will install Castaway so if you check your Video section Addons you will see it.

6. Now go back to SYSTEM then FILE MANAGER. On the left double click on Profile Directory. On the right select your flash drive again. Now RIGHT CLICK on favourites.xml and choose copy and click ok to confirm.

That's It ! Your favourites (STAR) in the bottom left of kodi now contains all live channels to watch at your convenience.

Note: There will be channels that will somedays work and somedays not. You may also experience some channels that are buffering alot, this is not a bad connection it's just a poor link that will be fixed. Keep in mind it's all free and you just have to take what you can get. When one channel doesn't work try it again in a few days and you may find it's back up.
